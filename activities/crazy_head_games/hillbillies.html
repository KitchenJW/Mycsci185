Hillbillies

On the surface, Hillbillies is a romp in the woods with your kin folk. However, as players dig deeper into this single-player or coop adventure, they will encounter a very lived-in world with some colorful and lovable characters. While Hillbillies takes a comical approach to some stereotypical perceptions of hill people, it also touches on some very real issues that affect people from varied cultures and backgrounds. Along with themes of tradition and legacy, Hillbillies also deals with addiction, mental illness, and abuse.The extensive dialogue system allows abundant player choice in how they interact with characters, and teaches that choices come with consequences. Players will learn communication skills and problem-solving techniques. Hillbillies also has elements of a management sim which gives players experience managing finances, developing organizational strategies, managing limited resources, and designing conservation techniques.

Players can choose one of three protagonists; Bubba, a very masculine feller; Bertha, a strong feminine force to be reckoned with; or Jesse, a gender-neutral oddball inventor determined to bring a little technology to the backwoods. There will be menial tasks to complete, problems to solve, and plenty of adventures to get into. So, role up your sleeves and your overalls and get ready for one heck of a good time.
